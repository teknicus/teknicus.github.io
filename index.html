Arduino Day 2K18




Arduino day is celebrated worldwide on 1st April by the Arduino community to raise awareness of the platform and the opportunities that it provides. The Birthday celebration of our favourite prototyping platform is celebrated in our campus by The Project Team and the electronics club of the Department of Electronics and Communication on 2nd and 3rd April.

Why Arduino Day?


This event offers an exclusive opportunity to get started with the platform if you haven't already and also to showcase your skills if you have. Being one of the core technical events on campus, this is a great opportunity to get a good view into the practical side of engineering.

How Can I Participate?


Just as the Arduino platform is a comprehensive package of both Hardware and Software, we wish to make the event accessible to beginners and amateurs alike. You can enroll yourself in workshops or attend the seminars to learn. The vast variety of events will give you the opportunity to showcase your skills and prove your mettle.

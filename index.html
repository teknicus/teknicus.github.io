About Us

JumpStarts was created with the sole aim of enabling people to JumpStart their career in trending domains of Computer Science, Electronics and IT engineering.

While MOOCs have allowed a wide audience to learn at their own pace, the experience of learning a subject live from someone who works with it on a regular basis is truly more rewarding.

Our Instructors are experienced students who have applied knowledge in the courses that they offer. Moreover, being students themselves, they are well aware of the needs of a beginner. This assures a great learning experience. for people of all skill levels.
